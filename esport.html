The Grid

Electronic sports, or Esports for short, is a new type of sport becoming quite popular. The concept of Esports is similar to that of mainstream sport.
Multiple parties are trying to win the game enabling the community to commit to a game or sport by watching other players compete on a high level in the game. 
Like in other sports, there are different types of genres. The most successful include strategy games, fighting games, and shooters.
These are mainly popular because they appeal to a big audience and have the highest prize money.
All genres are available to watch on Twitch and YouTube but can also sometimes get streamed live on television.

Short History

The first competition was in 1972.
At that time, it was just two
players competing over a

Now it has grown into a billion-dollar industry. We expect it to keep on this trend,

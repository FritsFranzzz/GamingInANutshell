The Health Effects of Gaming

Gaming is one of themost intense activities there is. Focusing and being exposed to stressfor a period must certainly have tremendous impacts.
Onthis page, we will take a closer look at just that!

Benefits

''Relaxing and having fun''
Gaming can be a great way to sit back andrelax,
therebyholding the ability to provide an escape of reality as well.
A recent report evenshows that over 40% of peoplegame.    

''Cognitive functions''
Playing video games has profound benefits for your brain.
Studies show that playing at least half an hour a day increases your focus and hand-eye coordination.
These benefits can come to use at school resulting in gamers having better grades than non-gamers.
Playing games also contribute to brain functions like creativity, perseverance and many more.              

  •  ''Motivational progression''
    Losing can help you to deal with failure. For this reason,
    we believe gaming and thus failing can help players deal more positively
    with real-life situations. Achieving your goals in games can also increase your self-esteem,
    increase determination and make you more positive


Risks

'Incorrect balance''
As seen above, gaming has many benefits if done in proportion.
Playing all day long prevents you from meeting new people and spending time doing school homework.
Games can also be quite expensive and contain certain types of gambling opponents which children should not get exposed to at a young age.
Gaming for extended periods is therefore not recommended.

''Game addiction''
Not being able to stop playing can have huge impacts on your daily life.
I have suffered from this type of addiction.
The worst part was the stress that it generates and the lack of human contact it brings with it.

''Physical problems''
Excessive use of videogames could lead to insufficient amounts of exercise, which is the cause of all sorts of problems.
Looking at a screen for multiple hours a day could also alter your sleep rhythm by preventing your brain from making melatonin which is one of the substances
responsible for making you feel sleepy.

''Conclusion''
Gaming holds the potential to be a great way to increase your overall happiness,
however, this can only be so when done in a balanced non-excessive way.

The market of games is continuing to grow, and we reckon this trend will keep up.

New games are getting produced every day with their player base growing with it.

On this page, we will take a closer look at the most popular genres that might dominate

the market. For this page, we asked YouTuber Brian Paul whether he thinks VR could be

a possible future for gaming. He has a successful channel with almost 75k subscribers

and could be considered an expert in the field.

 

Battle Royale: At current times, Battle Royale is a great attraction for both new and old gamers.

They now hold the main gamer-base. These are slow-paced games which mainly centre around

playing tactically and outlasting than the competition. While this these may be fun now, we do not

 reckon they will be around for long as they can grow tired on their community due to a lack of

 possible innovation.

 

Augmented Reality: While this is one of the newer genres, we think it holds the possibility

to be the next evolution of gaming. It allows players to look through their device and see

the world around them with added features. One famous example of this is ''Pokémon Go''

which works in a way that enables you to see the creatures around you through your mobile device.

 

Mobile Games: With over 2.4 billion active users, we reckon mobile games are here to stay.

Due to their low price point and the fact that almost everyone owns a phone, they make up

most of the gamers around the world. Recently, developers have been turning to mobile games

for because of this and games like Apex Legends will soon be playable on it and, this will generate an

even bigger player base.

eSports

Electronic sports, or eSports for short, is quite similar to that of mainstream sport.

Multiple parties are trying to win the game enabling the community

to commit to a game or sport by watching other players compete on a high level

in the game. Like in other sports, there are different types of genres.

The most successful include strategy games, fighting games, and shooters.

 These are mainly popular because they appeal to a big audience and have the

highest prize money. All genres are available to watch on Twitch and YouTube

but can also sometimes get streamed live on television.

Here we included a video of an Apex Legends tournament from last year.

In the video, you can notice that the mechanics are quite like ordinary sports
